Bio

Aayushman Parmar is a young, independent artist who launched his music career driven by pure inspiration. From writing horrorcore tracks to delivering lyrical blasts of freestyles, he is currently shaping his career with his debut studio album, ‘Manah Kalah’, a project that dives deep into the concepts and stories of his life.

Since the beginning, Aayushman’s goal has been to create music that resonates with the public on a meaningful level, moving beyond just "the vibe." Developing an interest in lyricism long before production, he writes with the intent for his words to hit the reader and for true Hip-Hop to be felt and enjoyed. While his production roots are in jazz rap and soul-sample styles, he has further delved into diverse soundscapes including luxury, soul, underground, and boom-bap.

His freestyle catalog includes ‘So Be It’, ‘Bodies’, and ‘cLOUDs’, all of which will soon be available on this website. Aayushman holds exclusive rights to his music, as every track is entirely self-produced.

His rapping inspirations include Eminem, Nas, J. Cole, Kendrick Lamar, The Notorious B.I.G., JID, MF DOOM, Denzel Curry, Wu-Tang Clan, and Mobb Deep. His production style is influenced by Tyler, The Creator, Pharrell Williams, No I.D., Thundercat, and J. Cole.

A staunch advocate for authenticity, Aayushman is a vocal opponent of AI-generated music. He actively spreads the message of human creativity, urging listeners not to let rising technologies become the primary medium of consumption. As a true student of the genre, he draws from the greats while carving out his own signature sound through a blend of Jazz-Rap, Rock-Rap, Boom-Bap, and Alternative Hip-Hop
